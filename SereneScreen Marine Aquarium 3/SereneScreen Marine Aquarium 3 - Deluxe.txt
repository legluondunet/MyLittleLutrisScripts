# SereneScreen Marine Aquarium 3 - Deluxe
### Description:
Enjoy this marvellous screensaver on Linux!
### Technical notes:
- keys:
-- right click or space: config options
-- B: turn bubbles sound on/off
-- F: Fullscreen/Windowed
-- S: Display statistics, framerate
-- ESC: exit
-- C: Displays the clock or calendar for 10 seconds. Pressing C repeatedly cycles through the visual elements
-- L: Show next image in the Logo playlist
More infos in the manual
- Known issues:
-- Bubbles audio cut
-- Music do not play
